Building Bridges Between Nations

Founded in 2023, IndoAsia Trading emerged from a vision to revolutionize trade between China and Bangladesh. We recognized the immense potential for Bangladeshi businesses to access high-quality Chinese products but saw the challenges they faced navigating international trade.

Our team combines decades of experience in international trade, logistics, and supply chain management. This deep understanding of both Chinese and Bangladeshi markets allows us to create seamless import experiences for our clients.

Today, we're proud to have facilitated over 500 successful imports while maintaining a 98% client satisfaction rate. Our commitment to transparency, quality, and efficiency has made us the preferred partner for SMEs across Bangladesh.
