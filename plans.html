Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our service plans

What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept all major credit cards, bank transfers, and PayPal. Enterprise clients may be eligible for net-30 payment terms.
Can I change my plan later? +
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. When upgrading, the new rate applies immediately. When downgrading, the new rate applies at the start of your next billing cycle.
Are there any hidden fees? +
No, our pricing is transparent. The only additional costs would be for services outside your selected plan, such as additional inspections or expedited shipping, which would be discussed and approved by you in advance.
Do you offer discounts for long-term commitments? +
Yes, we offer discounts for annual commitments. Professional and Enterprise plan subscribers can save up to 15% with annual billing.
What if I need to cancel my service? +
You can cancel your monthly plan at any time with 30 days' notice. Annual plans can be canceled with a prorated refund for unused months.
