General

We offer comprehensive import solutions including product sourcing, supplier verification, quality control, shipping logistics, customs clearance, and delivery to your doorstep in Bangladesh.

While we specialize in imports from China to Bangladesh, we can also facilitate imports from other Asian countries including Vietnam, Thailand, and Malaysia.

We implement a multi-stage quality control process including supplier vetting, pre-shipment inspections, and random sampling. Our local team in China conducts on-site inspections to ensure products meet your specifications.

Shipping & Logistics

We offer both sea freight and air freight options. Sea freight is more economical for larger shipments, while air freight is faster for urgent or smaller consignments. We recommend the best option based on your budget and timeline.

Shipping times vary based on the method: Air freight: 5-10 days, Sea freight: 20-35 days. These times include customs clearance at both origin and destination.

Yes, we provide complete customs clearance services in both China and Bangladesh. Our experienced team handles all documentation, duties, and taxes to ensure smooth clearance of your goods.

Payment & Pricing

We accept various payment methods including bank transfers, letters of credit, and online payments. For new clients, we typically require a deposit before order processing.

Our quotes include all costs: product cost, shipping, insurance, customs duties, taxes and our service fee. There are no hidden charges. We provide transparent breakdowns of all costs before you confirm your order.
