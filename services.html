Our Import Process

Simple, transparent, and efficient - from China to your doorstep

1

Consultation & Planning

We discuss your requirements, analyze market needs, and create a customized import strategy.

What's Included:
  • Market analysis and feasibility study
  • Product specification review
  • Budget planning and cost estimation
  • Timeline development
  • Risk assessment

Duration: 1-2 business days

2

Sourcing & Verification

Our team identifies and verifies the best suppliers, ensuring quality and competitive pricing.

Verification Process:
  • Factory audit and certification check
  • Financial stability assessment
  • Production capacity evaluation
  • Quality management system review
  • Reference and reputation verification

Duration: 3-5 business days

3

Order Processing

We handle all order details, negotiations, and documentation on your behalf.

Order Management:
  • Price negotiation and terms agreement
  • Purchase order creation and approval
  • Payment terms and schedule setup
  • Production timeline coordination
  • Documentation preparation

Duration: 2-3 business days

4

Payment Processing

Secure payment handling with flexible options and transparent transaction management.

Payment Options:
  • Bank transfer (T/T) - most common
  • Letter of Credit (L/C) for large orders
  • Trade assurance through Alibaba
  • Escrow services for added security
  • Flexible payment schedules (30% advance, 70% before shipment)

Duration: 1-2 business days

5

Quality Assurance

Thorough quality checks and inspections before shipment to ensure product standards.

Quality Control:
  • Pre-production sample approval
  • In-line production monitoring
  • Final random inspection (AQL standards)
  • Packaging and labeling verification
  • Detailed quality report generation

Duration: 1-2 days during production

6

Shipping & Tracking

Secure shipping with real-time tracking and regular updates throughout the journey.

Logistics Management:
  • Optimal shipping method selection
  • Container loading supervision
  • Real-time GPS tracking system
  • Regular status updates and notifications
  • Insurance coverage and documentation

Duration: 15-25 days (sea freight)

7

Customs & Delivery

Expert customs clearance and final delivery to your specified location in Bangladesh.

Final Delivery:
  • Customs documentation and clearance
  • Duty and tax calculation and payment
  • Port handling and container release
  • Final mile delivery coordination
  • Delivery confirmation and handover

Duration: 3-7 business days
